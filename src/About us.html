Founded in 1981, the TravelTime Family takes immense pleasure and pride to showcase their home to visitors from around the world. With such diversity and variety, this little island provides plentiful freedom opportunities for our professional and experienced team to cater to the visitors.
With many accolades received from around the world who have been mesmerized by the beauty of Sri Lanka and the hospitality of our people and the TravelTime team, we constantly seek to enhance it by carefully selecting other partner suppliers. Our mission is to ensure the best experience of this magnificent country, irrespective of budget.






Traveltime offer a complete Sri Lankan tour and travel package to travelers and travel agents. We have a passionate and professional team consisting of 40 representatives who possess extensive knowledge on the Sri Lanka tourism industry.
